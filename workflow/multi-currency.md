Sometimes an expense happens in a foreign currency — while traveling, or paying for an international service online. The app's base currency is TWD, but forcing manual conversion before logging creates friction and loses the original amount.

## Open Questions

- Should the app store both the original foreign amount and the TWD equivalent, or just the converted TWD amount?
- Who provides the exchange rate — user enters it manually, or the app fetches it automatically?
- Which currencies to support — common ones only (USD, EUR, JPY, CNY) or all?
- Does the expense list always show TWD, or can it show the original currency?
- How does this affect reports — are reports always in TWD?
